# **UIC Global Oculomics Initiative** {.home-hero}

*Building the world’s first multimodal foundational model for oculoplastic and craniofacial disease.* 
---

<div class="team-section">
  <h2>Basics</h2>
  <div class="team-list">

The UIC Global Oculomics Initiative is a research program focused on developing large multimodal datasets and foundational models capable of supporting oculoplastic and craniofacial diagnosis, global screening, and clinical decision support. The project integrates three major pillars:

<ul>
  <li><strong>Comprehensive clinical data from UIC</strong>, including full EHR histories, imaging archives, and curated multimodal cohorts.</li>
  <li><strong>Large-scale external datasets</strong>, including millions of eye- and face-region images with automatically extracted periorbital measurements.</li>
  <li><strong>Global health deployments</strong>, where standardized imaging and metadata collection are performed using the <a href="https://glorbit.app">Glorbit</a> platform across diverse international sites.</li>
</ul>

By combining clinical data, massive pretraining datasets, and prospective global cohorts, this initiative aims to establish a reproducible foundation for next-generation oculoplastics AI.
